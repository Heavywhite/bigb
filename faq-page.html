üì¶ Orders

How do I place an order? +

To place an order on BigB:

  • Browse products and click "Add to Cart" on items you want
  • Click the cart icon to review your items
  • Click "Proceed to Checkout"
  • Enter your delivery address and contact information
  • Select your preferred payment method
  • Review your order and click "Place Order"

You'll receive an order confirmation via email and SMS.

Can I modify or cancel my order? +

Yes, you can modify or cancel your order within 2 hours of placing it, as long as it hasn't been processed yet. To cancel:

  • Go to "My Orders" in your account
  • Find the order you want to cancel
  • Click "Cancel Order" and confirm

If your order has already been processed or shipped, please contact customer support at +256 700 000 000.

How do I track my order? +

You can track your order in several ways:

  • Visit our Track Order page and enter your order ID
  • Log into your account and go to "My Orders"
  • Click the tracking link in your shipping confirmation email

You'll receive real-time updates on your order status.

What is the minimum order value? +

There is no minimum order value at BigB. You can order any product regardless of price. However, orders above UGX 500,000 qualify for free delivery.

üí≥ Payment

What payment methods do you accept? +

We accept the following payment methods:

  • MTN Mobile Money: Pay directly from your MoMo wallet
  • Airtel Money: Secure Airtel Money payments
  • Credit/Debit Cards: Visa and Mastercard accepted
  • Cash on Delivery: Pay when you receive your order (available in Kampala and major cities)
Is it safe to pay online? +

Yes, absolutely! We use industry-standard security measures:

  • 256-bit SSL encryption for all transactions
  • Secure payment gateways (Paystack/Flutterwave)
  • PCI DSS compliant payment processing
  • No storage of complete card details on our servers

Your payment information is always protected.

Why was my payment declined? +

Payments can be declined for several reasons:

  • Insufficient funds in your account/wallet
  • Incorrect card details or expired card
  • Bank security restrictions on online payments
  • Daily transaction limit reached
  • Network connectivity issues

If your payment is declined, please try again or contact your bank/mobile money provider. You can also try an alternative payment method.

Do you offer installment payments? +

Currently, we do not offer installment payment options. However, we're working on partnerships with payment providers to offer "Buy Now, Pay Later" services in the near future. Stay tuned for updates!

üöö Shipping & Delivery

What are your delivery times? +

Delivery times vary based on your location:

  • Kampala: Same day or next day delivery
  • Entebbe, Mukono: 1-2 business days
  • Jinja, Mbarara, other major cities: 2-3 business days
  • Remote areas: 3-5 business days

Express delivery is available in Kampala for an additional fee.

How much does shipping cost? +

Shipping costs:

  • Standard Delivery: UGX 15,000
  • Express Delivery (Kampala): UGX 25,000
  • FREE Delivery: Orders above UGX 500,000

Shipping cost is calculated at checkout based on your location.

Do you deliver on weekends? +

Yes, we deliver Monday through Saturday. Deliveries are made between 8:00 AM and 6:00 PM. Sunday deliveries are not currently available, but orders placed on weekends will be processed on Monday.

Can I change my delivery address? +

Yes, you can change your delivery address before the order is dispatched. Contact our customer support immediately at +256 700 000 000 or email support@bigb.ug with your order ID and new address. Once the order is out for delivery, the address cannot be changed.

üîÑ Returns & Refunds

What is your return policy? +

We offer a 7-day return policy from the date of delivery. Items must be:

  • Unused and in original condition
  • In original packaging with all tags attached
  • Accompanied by the original receipt/invoice

Visit our Returns Policy page for detailed information.

How do I return a product? +

To return a product:

  • Log into your account and go to "My Orders"
  • Select the order containing the item you want to return
  • Click "Request Return" and select the item(s)
  • Provide a reason for the return
  • Our team will review and approve your request
  • Package the item securely and wait for pickup
When will I receive my refund? +

Refunds are processed within 3-5 business days after we receive and inspect the returned item. The refund will be credited to your original payment method:

  • Mobile Money: 1-2 business days
  • Card Payments: 5-7 business days
  • Cash on Delivery: Bank transfer within 3-5 business days
Can I exchange an item? +

Yes, exchanges are available for items in different sizes or colors. To exchange an item, initiate a return request and specify that you want an exchange. Once we receive the returned item, we'll ship the replacement at no additional cost. Exchanges are subject to stock availability.

üë§ Account Management

Do I need an account to shop? +

While you can browse products without an account, you need to create an account to place orders. Having an account allows you to:

  • Track your orders in real-time
  • Save your delivery addresses
  • View order history
  • Save items to your wishlist
  • Receive exclusive offers and promotions
  • Manage returns and refunds easily
How do I reset my password? +

To reset your password:

  • Click "Login" in the top navigation
  • Click "Forgot Password?"
  • Enter your registered email or phone number
  • You'll receive a password reset link via email or SMS
  • Click the link and create a new password

If you don't receive the reset link, check your spam folder or contact support.

How do I update my account information? +

To update your account information:

  • Log into your account
  • Click on your profile name in the top right
  • Select "Account Settings"
  • Update your name, email, phone number, or password
  • Click "Save Changes"

You can also manage your delivery addresses in the same section.

Can I delete my account? +

Yes, you can request account deletion by contacting our customer support at support@bigb.ug. Please note that:

  • All pending orders must be completed or cancelled first
  • Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone
  • Your order history will be archived but not accessible
  • Any unused credits or vouchers will be forfeited

‚ùì General Questions

What is BigB? +

BigB is Uganda's premier e-commerce platform that connects consumers with quality products from trusted sellers across the country. We offer a wide range of products including electronics, fashion, home goods, beauty products, and more, with secure payment options and fast delivery.

Are the products genuine? +

Yes, all products sold on BigB are 100% genuine. We work directly with authorized distributors and verified sellers. Every product comes with a quality guarantee and manufacturer warranty where applicable. If you receive a counterfeit product, we offer a full refund and investigate the seller immediately.

How do I contact customer support? +

You can reach our customer support team through:

  • Phone: +256 700 000 000 (Mon-Sat, 8AM-8PM)
  • Email: support@bigb.ug
  • Live Chat: Click the chat icon on our website
  • WhatsApp: +256 700 000 000

Our support team responds within 24 hours.

Do you have physical stores? +

Currently, BigB operates exclusively online. This allows us to offer competitive prices and wider product selection. However, we're planning to open pickup locations in Kampala where customers can collect their orders. Stay tuned for announcements!

How do I apply a promo code? +

To apply a promo code:

  • Add items to your cart and proceed to checkout
  • On the cart page, find the "Promo Code" field
  • Enter your promo code and click "Apply"
  • The discount will be reflected in your order total

Promo codes cannot be combined and have specific terms and conditions.
