üìã Return Policy Overview

At BigB, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy with your order, you can return it within 7 days of delivery for a full refund or exchange.

‚úì What You Can Return

Most items can be returned within 7 days of delivery, provided they meet our return conditions.

Eligible for Return:

  • Items that are defective or damaged upon arrival
  • Products that don't match the description or images
  • Wrong items delivered
  • Items that are not the right size or color (for fashion items)
  • Products with manufacturing defects
  • Items you simply changed your mind about (within 7 days)

Not Eligible for Return:

  • Items returned after 7 days from delivery date
  • Products that have been used, worn, or damaged by you
  • Items without original packaging, tags, or accessories
  • Perishable goods (food, flowers, etc.)
  • Personal care items (cosmetics, underwear, etc.) that have been opened
  • Custom-made or personalized items
  • Digital downloads or gift cards

üîÑ How to Return an Item

Follow these simple steps to return your item:

1

Go to "My Orders" and find the order containing the item you want to return.

2

Our team will review your request within 24 hours and send you a confirmation email.

Once approved, securely pack the item in its original packaging with all accessories.

5

We'll arrange a free pickup from your location. Our courier will collect the package.

6

Get Your Refund

After we receive and inspect the item, your refund will be processed within 3-5 business days.
