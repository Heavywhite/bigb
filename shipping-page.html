‚ùì Common Questions

Can I change my delivery address?

Yes, contact us immediately at +256 700 000 000 before your order is dispatched. Address changes cannot be made once the order is out for delivery.

What if I'm not home during delivery?

Our driver will attempt to contact you. If unsuccessful, the package will be held for 24 hours and a second delivery attempt will be made. You may also arrange for someone else to receive it.

Do you deliver on weekends?

Yes, we deliver Monday through Saturday between 8:00 AM and 6:00 PM. Sunday deliveries are not currently available.

How do I track my order?

Use your order ID to track your package on our Track Order page. You'll also receive SMS updates at every stage.

What about fragile items?

All fragile items are specially packed and marked. Our delivery partners are trained to handle them with extra care.
